Null space

#ilasem1 Created: 2021-11-10 06:39

The nulls space of $A$ can be denoted as:

null $A = {x \in \mathbb{R}^n | Ax = 0}$

In other words, all solutions of $x$ in the homogeneous system $Ax = 0$
